Description

I am Jonas Valstar, 19, and that guy you see to the right of this text, that is also me. I am a programmer that also likes 3d modelling, making Photoshop edits and is interested in making movies currently studying at the Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen.


Ever since I can remember i have been playing video games. Either on the Playstation 2, Nintendo WII or the computer. According to my parents and friends that know me since I was a little kid, I have also always said that i wanted to make video games when i grow up. And that is exactly what i am planning to do. This love for video games I can trace back all the way to playing Skylanders: Giants for the first time at a friends house.


Currently I am mostly busy finding ways to make something that I have never done in school assignments to keep learning new skills. I always tend to overdo almost anything when am passionate about doing it while staying within the given time. I am also quite a prefectionist and, when allowed to, will keep improving something until i can't find any small mistake anymore or the deadline is in 5 minutes.
