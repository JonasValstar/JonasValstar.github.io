Description

I am Jonas Valstar, 19, and that guy you see to the right of this text, that is also me. I am a programmer that also likes to dabble his toes in other aspects of game-design, like 3D modelling, and is currently studying at the Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen. So far, I love procedural things and algorithms the most. But I also love doing any other kind of programming.


Ever since I can remember, I have been playing video games. Either on the PlayStation 2 & 3, Nintendo WII or the computer. According to my parents and friends that have known me since I was a little kid, I have also always said that I wanted to make video games when I grew up. And that is exactly what I am planning to do. This love of video games I can trace back all the way to playing Skylanders: Giants for the first time at a friend’s house.


I like trying to do things I have never done before to broaden my experience and to learn new skills. The more difficult such a new thing is, the better. I always strive to achieve the highest quality possible in what I make, and I try to make sure it is understandable for, and usable by, other people.


Lastly, I would say that maybe my best quality is that I don’t need mountains of coffee every day to function. Some tea is appreciated though.
