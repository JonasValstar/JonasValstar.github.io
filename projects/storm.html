In this project, I wanted to learn VR development. It was the first time I worked with VR, so it was all still new to me. I also wanted to use the mechanics unique to VR in an interesting way based on the Pixel ripped games.


With this I suggested to my team that we made a puzzle game that would see the player play 2D games that would only be completable by interacting with it in VR.

Early version of Level 1 mechanics

In level 1 I made a system where the player would control a character in a dungeon fighting enemies. The VR aspect here came in the fact that there was a map laid out behind the player that the player could interact with. For example, they had to put a notebook on the bottomless pit, pour a glass of water on a lava-lake or use put a key on a door to open it. This would be seen back in the 2D game causing the player to progress again.

Level 2 Design

In level 2 was music concert inspired, so I made a system where the player would play a 2D game on the concert screens. They had to control the lights, smoke- and fire machines to help their 2D character. The player would also have to dodge the concert lasers.
