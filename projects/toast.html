toast

/

As homework for my studies I had to make a model of a toaster and a blender. I decided to overdo this and make a whole robot character out of it. I like that model so much that i ended up making an animation with him as well. For both modelling and animating, but also lighting, camera and story this was the first time i really started doing it. I ended up liking it a lot, that i continued working on the animation. The current plan is to make a (very) short movie of it that will be around 5 minutes. I have now completed the first scene, and i started working on the second one.
