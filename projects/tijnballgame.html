tijnballgame

/

For a presentation school assignment we decided to make a simple game that we could use as examples. I did all the programming of the game and make the ball bounce in a realistic way of off the bar. I also added wind into the game which would influence the ball in the air along with a hoop that would reward the player more points if they got the ball through.


Design Process

I first made the hoop follow the mouse along with tilting slightly based on the amount it moved from the center. I then added the ball and made it bounce from the bar according the initial velocity and angle of the bar. After that i made the wind that influences the ball while it is in the air. It starts of at either 0.5 or -0,5 speed and randomly increases or decreases over time. I also added block moving around the speed of the wind to indicate to the player how fast it was going. Lastly i added the hoops that give the player a lot of points. It works by checking the if the ball entered it collider. When the ball after that exits again, it checks if the entry point was higher than the exit point and rewards the points if it was.
